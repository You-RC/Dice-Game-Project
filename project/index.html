Project - Dice Game

How to play

The game is simple. Click the "Roll Dice" button to roll the dice. To advance to the next round, click the "Roll Dice" button again. After three rounds, the game will display your final score. If you want to play again, click the "New Game" button or refresh the page.

Rules

Each round, you and the computer both roll two dices. The sum of the two dice will be added to your score. If you roll doubles then your score is multiplied by 2 (e.g. a 6 and a 6 then your score is 24 points). Rolling a 1 will give you a score of 0 for that round. The player with the highest score after three rounds wins.
